    fix(sglang): gate Mooncake HIP transport for cross-node PD KV transfer
    
    The v0.5.15.post1 base bundles Mooncake at upstream #2682 ("rdma+hip
    multi-protocol segments for single-node disaggregation"), which installs a
    HIP transport unconditionally and registers the KV pool under both rdma and
    hip. selectTransport then prefers hip (priority 4 > rdma 2), so even a
    CROSS-NODE transfer uses HIP IPC — hipIpcOpenMemHandle can't open a peer's
    handle across hosts, so decode never receives KV (500, gibberish).
    
    Older bases predated #2682 (no HIP transport at all), so cross-node PD used
    RDMA and worked. This regressed with the base bump.
    
    Fix: patch_mooncake_sglang.sh gates the HIP install behind MC_ENABLE_HIP_TRANSPORT
    (default OFF) and rebuilds the engine in place, then Dockerfile.sglang runs it.
    The rebuild pins the target arch (gfx950) so it works in the GPU-less docker
    build (ROCm's amdgpu-arch probe fails without a device and would otherwise drop
    the HIP engine target). Verified end-to-end on chi2879+chi2865: DSv4 sglang PD
    1p1d cross-node smoke passes on BOTH mooncake and mori after the fix.
